❓ FAQ ❓

Got questions? We got answers

How long until I get my rank after buying? ▼
Once you complete the PayPal payment and verify your username, our team is notified via Discord instantly. Most ranks are applied within 5–15 minutes. If the server is offline when you buy, it'll be applied the next time you join.
Is Trial Mod a real staff rank or just a donor title? ▼
Trial Mod is a real staff rank with actual permissions — /mute, /kick, spectator mode, and a private staff chat. You are expected to use your powers responsibly. Abuse will result in removal without a refund.
What if the server is offline when I buy? ▼
No problem — your rank is queued and will be applied as soon as the server comes back online. Join our Discord to track the status or ping a staff member.
Can I get a refund? ▼
All purchases are final and non-refundable once applied. If there's a technical issue — rank not applied after 24 hours — contact us on Discord and we'll sort it.
Do ranks survive server resets? ▼
Yes. Ranks are stored permanently and tied to your Minecraft username — they survive any server resets or map wipes.
Can I buy a rank for a friend? ▼
Absolutely. Go to the Player Lookup tab, search their username, and hit "Buy Trial Mod for this Player" — the checkout will use their username automatically.
What payment methods are accepted? ▼
We use PayPal. You can pay with your PayPal balance, credit card, or debit card through PayPal's checkout — no account required for card payments.
How do I join the server? ▼
The server IP is E1BoxLife.minehut.gg — click the IP at the top of the page to copy it. You need Minecraft Java Edition. If the server is sleeping, it may take 30 seconds to wake up on first join.
