FAQ

Everything you need to know

How long does production take?▼
All items are made to order. Standard production is 7–14 days after your payment and design are confirmed. Custom bespoke orders may take longer — we'll give you a timeline on Discord.
How does ordering work?▼
Click Order on any item, enter your contact info, complete PayPal payment, then we'll DM you on Discord to confirm size, colour, and delivery address.
Do you ship internationally?▼
Yes — we ship worldwide. Shipping cost is added at checkout based on your location. Most UK orders arrive within 3–5 days of dispatch. International is 7–21 days.
Can I return or exchange an item?▼
Because all items are custom made to order, we don't accept returns unless the item arrives damaged or incorrect. Contact us on Discord within 48 hours of delivery if there's an issue.
Can I send my own design?▼
100% — DM us on Discord with your artwork (PNG/SVG preferred). We'll check it's print-ready and give you a quote on the same day.
What payment methods are accepted?▼
We accept PayPal — you can pay with your balance, debit card, or credit card through PayPal's checkout. No account required for card payments.
