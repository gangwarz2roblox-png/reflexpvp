FAQ

How does delivery work? +
After payment, you'll receive an Order ID. Our team verifies your payment and sends you a verification code. Enter it on the site to confirm your order.
What payment methods do you accept? +
We accept PayPal and Revolut / Bank Transfer.
How long does delivery take? +
Most orders are verified and delivered within 1–24 hours. Join our Discord to track your order or message support.
Can I get a refund? +
Due to the nature of in-game items, we do not offer refunds once delivered. Contact us on Discord before purchasing if unsure.
What game are these items for? +
All items are for Trap Town on Roblox. Make sure you have the game and a Roblox account to receive your items.
How do I contact support? +
Join our Discord server and open a support ticket. We respond within a few hours.
