❓ FAQ ❓

Got questions? We got answers

How long until I get my item in-game?▼
After payment and entering your Roblox username, our team is notified instantly. Most items are delivered within a few minutes. If you're offline it'll be given next time you join.
How does ordering work?▼
Click Buy Now on any item, enter your Roblox username, complete PayPal — then our team gives you the item in-game. Done.
What game are these items for?▼
All items are for our Roblox game. Cars spawn in your in-game garage, clothing gets applied to your avatar inside the game. Join our Discord for the game link.
Can I get a refund?▼
All purchases are final once delivered in-game. If there's a technical issue and you didn't receive your item after 24 hours, contact us on Discord.
Can I request a custom design?▼
Yes — DM us on Discord with what you want. Custom items start from £25.
What payment methods are accepted?▼
We accept PayPal — pay with balance, debit card, or credit card. No PayPal account required for card payments.
